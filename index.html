We Are the Jason Aguirre Group

Built for the buyers most agents send away

A lot of Houston families have been told they can't own a home — not yet, not with that credit, not without a Social. We hear that story every week, and most of the time it simply isn't true. It just needs someone who knows the path and takes the time to walk it with you.

We pair you with lenders who work with ITIN borrowers and first-time programs, build a realistic plan when your credit needs a few months, and never rush you into a payment that keeps you up at night.

You work directly with Jason and a small, bilingual team — not a call center. When you text, a real person who knows your file texts back.
